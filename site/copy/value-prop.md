# Value Proposition

**Stop losing signal between tools.**

- **Eval data drives fine-tuning:** Slancha captures production signal during inference and routes it directly into your fine-tuning pipeline — no manual exports, no lost context, no broken handoffs between eval and training teams.

- **Optimize across three dimensions simultaneously:** Benchmark cost, latency, and accuracy against your real workloads, then deploy across API, hosted, or on-prem inference. Slancha optimizes on all three at once, not just one.

- **Compounding improvement by design:** Each eval → deploy → post-train cycle produces a better model at lower cost. Slancha tracks gains across cycles so you see the compounding effect — not just snapshot metrics.
