# About Slancha

## Empowering Inference at the Speed of Business

Slancha is a latency‑first inference platform that lets you focus on building great AI products while we handle the heavy lifting of model optimization, hardware selection, and cost management. Our control plane intelligently routes workloads across best‑in‑class infrastructure—whether it’s AWS Inferentia, NVIDIA L40S, or custom GPU clusters—so you always hit your SLA without overpaying.

### Why Choose Slancha?
- **Latency‑First Architecture** – We start with the latency target you need and work backwards to pick the optimal stack.
- **Hardware Agnostic** – Seamlessly switch between Inferentia, L40S, B200, or any compatible accelerator without rewriting code.
- **Zero Ops Overhead** – No inference team required; we become your dedicated SRE for AI workloads.

### Our Mission
We believe that every organization should be able to run production‑grade inference at scale without the complexity and expense of managing hardware. By automating the decisions that matter most, Slancha lets you deliver faster, cheaper, and more reliable AI experiences.
